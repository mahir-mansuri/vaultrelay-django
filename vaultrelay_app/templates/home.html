How VaultRelay Works

Our simple three-step process ensures your documents are always protected and accessible to the right people.

1

Upload Your Documents

Securely upload your important documents to your private VaultRelay storage. All files are encrypted before they leave your device.

2

Designate a Trusted Contact

Choose a trusted person and set a secret code that they'll need to access your documents if needed. You control the timeframe of inactivity before transfer.

3

Automatic Backup Protocol

If you don't log in or show activity within your specified timeframe, your documents are automatically sent to your trusted contact when they provide the secret code.
