VAULT ACCESS RECOVERY

⚠ Locked out? Follow these steps to regain access to your digital vault.
1
Reset via Registered Email

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive a reset link on your registered email.
🔹 Didn't get it? Check your spam or junk folder.

2
Verify Using OTP

Enter the OTP sent to your email or mobile number to verify your identity.
🔹 Still not receiving OTP? Check SMS inbox or wait a few minutes.

3
Set a New Password

Create a strong new password (e.g., MyPass@2025).
✔ Use uppercase, lowercase, number & symbol.
✔ Save it in a password manager or write it down safely.
🚫 Never share your password with anyone.
